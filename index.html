I build digital infrastructure for markets that English-language platforms ignore.

Currently: MojaCard — a virtual Visa card that lets Angolans and the African diaspora load in local currency and spend globally. No bank account required. The settlement architecture routes around Angola's FX capital controls using stablecoins — legally, today, with no central bank licence needed.

Also running Naxela Digital — social media management, AI-powered WhatsApp bots, and web infrastructure for businesses across the UK, Cabo Verde, Angola, and Portugal. And CustodyLog — a cryptographic chain-of-custody SaaS for forensic laboratories, with client-side SHA-256 hashing so evidence files never leave the browser.

Before this: 12 peer-reviewed publications in BMJ, The Lancet, and Nature Communications. Database administrator for Angola's national AFIS fingerprint identification system. PCR and microbiology scientist across NHS and UKHSA labs during COVID-19. BSc Biomedicine with honours, Birkbeck / University of London.

The common thread: systems, data, and the spaces between markets where things don't work yet.
